Digital Read Out Technolgoy Based on Open Standards and Designs

Digital Scales

The OpenDRO solution is compatible with commercially available quadrature products as well as an open-source design which can be built for under $25 per axis, easily mountable and expandable to nearly any length. Development is in-progress to support iGaging, DigiMag, AccuRemote, Shahe and other Mixed Scale products.

Hardware

The open-sourced hardware design includes a PCB layout, bluetooth connectivity readily available components. The arduino ecosystem far surpasses other microcontrollers which makes the solution less expensive and more widely available.

App

Fully-compatible with the proprietary TouchDRO app for android, the OpenDRO Project intends to develop an open source app to expand functionality as well as extending to the iPad platform.
